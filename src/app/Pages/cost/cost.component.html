Discounts

2018 Student discount

Any individual who is enrolled on a SAE Diploma course will receive a 10% discount on the published Short Course fee when they enroll on any Short Course.

Friend discount

Two individuals or more that enrol together on a Short Course will each receive a 5% discount on the published Short Course fee.


Only one discount may be used per person and the above discounts may not be combined with one another or any other offer.
